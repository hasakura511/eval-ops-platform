Executive Cockpit Governance control panel for policy envelope and rule health. Use to decide whether to tighten or relax oversight.

Global Status Overall system status classification. Colors indicate nominal, warning, or critical states.   —

Time Timestamp for the current telemetry snapshot (ISO-8601). Confirms data freshness.

—

Iteration Monotonic loop counter for governance cycles. Higher means later iteration.

—

Command Latest executive action issued to the system (e.g., FREEZE, HALT). Indicates current operating mode.

—

Policy Envelope Core governance parameters that tune audit coverage, process budget, and risk appetite.

Audit Probability (p) Fraction of outputs selected for audit. Higher p increases oversight but adds cost. —
Process Budget (β) Upper bound on governance overhead. Exceeding β triggers deregulation protocols. —
Risk Appetite (r) Acceptable risk threshold for outputs. Lower r means stricter acceptance criteria. —

Rule Health Signals about rule churn and complexity. Higher entropy or budget usage may indicate governance drift.

Rule Entropy Measure of rule complexity and volatility. Higher entropy indicates a less stable ruleset. —
Rule Budget Current rule count versus allowed budget (b). Higher usage leaves less room for new rules. —

Deregulation Trigger Alert fired when AdminRatio exceeds β. Indicates the system should reduce governance overhead.
