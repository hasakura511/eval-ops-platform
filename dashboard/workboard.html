Workboard screen.workboard
Purpose
Portrait triage view for deciding which runs need attention now.
Inputs
  • Snapshot core fields as_of, health.
  • Run list projects[].tracks[].runs[].
  • Todos and alerts runs[].todos[], alerts[].
Output / Action
  • Pick which run to open, assign, or escalate.
  • Decide whether the system is healthy enough to proceed.

  • Metrics may be undefined or inconsistent across tracks.

Why this screen exists

Two-monitor intent: portrait shows the triage list (this screen), while ultrawide shows ownership and hierarchy.

Learn in 10 seconds: which runs are failing, who owns them, and what the next action is.

Next action: pick the top failing run, check artifacts, and assign a human or agent to unblock it.

Assumptions / Open Questions

  • What does health.status mean and who computes it?
  • What is the canonical definition of failure_count?
